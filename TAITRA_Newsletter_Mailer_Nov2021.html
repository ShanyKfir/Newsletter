Israel - the Unicorn-Nation
Due to COVID-19, which has highlighted the importance of innovation and technology to businesses, as well as low-interest rates, record investments have been made in technology firms globally, leading to the creation of tech unicorns, private companies valued at at least $1 billion.

Israel has become a production line for unicorns growing as Israeli companies in a push to create large and hopefully profitable businesses while transforming the Israeli technology landscape.

Here are two Israeli unicorns we chose to highlight.
 
Claroty is an industrial cybersecurity company. Trusted by the world’s largest enterprises, Claroty helps customers reveal, protect, and manage their OT, IoT, and IIoT assets, providing a full range of industrial cybersecurity controls for visibility, threat detection, risk, and vulnerability management.

Melio is on a mission to keep small businesses in business by providing a smart B2B payment solution tailor-made for their needs. Melio's digital accounts payable and receivable dashboard provides a single, integrated tool that allows small businesses to transfer and receive payments quickly and easily.
